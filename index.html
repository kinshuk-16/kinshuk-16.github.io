About me

I’m currently a master’s student at UC Berkeley’s School of Information with a focus on user centric product design and development.

I did my undergrad in computer science engineering and worked for two years in Oracle Cloud Services as system admin for cloud architecture.

At Berkeley I am learning to look at technology from engineering as well as from a social and behavioural lens. I am exploring UI design and development, UX research, data analysis and visualization. I aim to combine my technical skills with knowledge of human centric design and decision making.

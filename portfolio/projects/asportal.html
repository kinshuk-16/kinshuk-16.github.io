 Assignment Portal

Developed as a part of class project for Advanced Internet Technology class in a team of two with Reeth Dhar.

Problem:

We wanted to make a platform for instructors to give assignments and quizzes to class and for students to keep a record of their grades in all the assignemnts and quizzes.

Process:

  • We noted that there was no place for professors to give quizzes or assignments or a centralized datatbase where students can keep track of quiz and assignment scores.
  • We started with talking to professors to discuss the type of portal that they could use to give out assignments. We discussed the features that should be available for instructors and students.
  • We tallked to our classmates regarding the need for a centralized database with scores on it.
  • Based on the interviews we came up with a list of features we wanted to include in such a platform.
  • We developed the portal which included feature for instructor to add assignment or add a pool of questions through which quizes woud be randomly generated. Students could log in and complete the quizes or assigned work and look at their previous scores.

Technologies used:

ASP.NET, VB, HTML, CSS, SQL
